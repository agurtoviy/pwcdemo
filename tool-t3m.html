Tax Management Maturity Model (T3M)

Tax authorities increasingly expect organisations to be able to confirm that tax risks are appropriately managed in a way that facilitates real time compliance and data oriented auditing. These challenges are combined with demands for increased transparency from a wide range of stakeholders, and all have to be faced by organisations while managing tax risk and opportunity in a cost effective way.

Our Tax Management Maturity Model (T3M) allows you to understand and analyse the tax control framework and current level of tax risk and opportunity management within your tax function. Here we provide My Taxpartner members with an abridged version of T3M for you to:

  • Self-assess your current and desired state of tax management effectiveness.
  • Communicate to your key stakeholders the maturity of your tax function, be it internal audit, corporate finance, external auditors or tax administrations around the globe. 
  • If you wish, use your self-assessment - you can print this out on completion of the questionnaire - to start a conversation with your usual PwC tax advisor. We'll then work with you (on a basis to be agreed) to challenge and guide your ambition level and provide recommendations and detailed action plan for improvement.
